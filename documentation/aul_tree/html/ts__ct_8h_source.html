35  _____________________________________________________________________________
36  Description:
37  Compile Time Time Stamp (CTTS). The purpose is primarily to automate The
38  time stamping of any source that has been compiled, and the time stamp
39  provides a unique serialized sequence of software revisions that are
40  released out to the wild. The format provides a unique, not repeating
41  serial number (to the resolution you might need) that serves both as a
42  time stamp and/or sequential serial number.
43 
44  Converts __DATE__ and __TIME__ to particular time stamp formats.
45 
46  01234567890
47  __DATE__ MMM DD YYYY M=month, D=Day, Y=Year
48  __TIME__ HH:NN:SS H=hours, N=minutes, S=seconds
